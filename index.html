What we do?

We offer you quality and love in Natural Touch

Natural Touch Aesthetics in Plymouth-Devon offers anti-wrinkle Injections of botulinum toxin (Botox). Derma fillers and Lip augmentation. It is run by a qualified nurse certified by Harley Academy and member of Royal College of Nursing who provides the best and most effective aesthetic treatments suitable to your individual needs.

Work methodology

At Natural Touch we follow these 5 steps:

1. Initial consultation

We listen and talk about what it is you want to achieve, and discuss the best ways we can help.

2. Before your treatment

We will take you through the procedure and explain what's going to happen. You will be able to ask the nurse any questions.

3. The treatment

We will make certain that you are completely comfortable before the treatment starts.

4. Post-treatment

We will tell you if you are likely to be in any discomfort after your treatment. We can advise you on which painkillers to take if necessary.

5. Aftercare

We will advise you on how best to care for your new look in addition to advising you on any aftercare or precautions to take to help you maintain your new look.
